"Tabs" (or Tablature), is a simplified way to notate harmonica solos, harmonica parts, melodies and songs, without having to formally read music. See the Tabs main page for more information. These tabs are the author's (submitter's) own work and represents their interpretation of the song or riff. You may only use these tabs for private study, scholarship, or research.

  • Timing for the Tabs is not included- You will note that the timing is not available for the tablature. If you don't know the harmonica part or melody well, you may find it difficult to play.     4B = hole 4 blow        6D = hole 6 draw
    3,4,5B = holes 3, 4, and 5 blow together

    4D-b (lower case "B") = bent note by one half-step
    2D-bb (2 lower case "B"s) = bent note by two half-steps
    {sus} = sustain (hold) note
    4/5Dwarb = warble (trill) between 4 and 5 draw
    2D---5D = slide from hole 2 to 5 draw 
"Basic Country" riff-   [Intermediate level]
  Diatonic harmonica in 2nd position:

3D-bb     3D     4D{sus}

3D-bb     3D     4D     3D-bb     3D-b     3D-bb     2D{sus}
"Country #2" riff-   [Intermediate level]
  Diatonic harmonica, 2nd position:

1D     2B     2D     3D     4D{sus}

1D     2B     2D     3D     3D-bb     2D{sus}
"Country #3" riff-   [Intermediate level]
  Diatonic harmonica, 2nd position:

3D-bb    3D     4D     5B     6B{sus}

7D         6D     6B     5B     4D{sus}
